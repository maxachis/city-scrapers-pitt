City-County Task Force on Disabilities Meetings

The City-County Task Force on Disabilities is moving virtual! To keep our communities healthy and safe while continuing to meet, the Task Force will now meet via virtual meeting on Zoom as well as stream the meeting on YouTube.

If you are not planning to question/comment, please watch the YouTube Live stream at https://www.youtube.com/c/PittsburghCityPlanning to allow those with questions or comments to be able to join the meeting.

To join the Zoom meeting, click on the link below and you will be added as an attendee. This will allow you to watch the meeting, but will not allow you to speak until called upon. If you would like to speak on a topic, please use the “raise hand” function to signify to the host that you would like to speak. You will be called on and given the opportunity to present at that time.

  • Join the Zoom meeting with this link: https://us02web.zoom.us/j/83666489136
  • Join by phone by calling +1 301 715 8592  with Webinar ID: 836 6648 9136

Anyone who requires an accommodation for effective communication or a modification of policies or procedures to participate in a program, service or activity provided by the City of Pittsburgh should contact the City ADA Coordinator as soon as possible but no later than two business days before the event.

Contact: Hillary Roman, City of Pittsburgh ADA Coordinator, Hillary.Roman@pittsburghpa.gov; Remote Ph: (412) 301-7041

 

To receive updates and announcements about the City-County Task Force on Disabilities via email, please send your information to Hillary Roman at Hillary.Roman@pittsburghpa.gov.
